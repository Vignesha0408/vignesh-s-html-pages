Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services

What design services do you offer?

I offer a comprehensive range of design services including brand identity, web design, UI/UX design, illustration, print design, and marketing materials. Each project is tailored to meet your specific needs and goals.

How long does a typical project take?

Timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. A logo design typically takes 1-2 weeks, while a full brand identity package takes 3-4 weeks. Web design projects range from 4-8 weeks. I always provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What information do you need to start a project?

I'll need to understand your business, target audience, brand values, and specific requirements. A brief document outlining your goals, preferences, and any reference materials you like will help kickstart the project effectively.

Do you provide source files?

Yes, I provide all source files upon project completion. This includes vector files, design files, and any necessary documentation to ensure you have full control over your assets.

What's your revision policy?

I include up to 3 rounds of revisions in most projects to ensure you're completely satisfied. Additional revisions are available at an hourly rate. My goal is to deliver work that exceeds your expectations.
